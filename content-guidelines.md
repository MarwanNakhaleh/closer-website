# Closer — Content Guidelines

Writing guidelines that apply to all Closer content: blog posts, TikTok scripts, marketing copy, and website text.

## AI Tells to Avoid

These patterns are widely recognized as AI-generated writing and will undermine trust with readers:

- Avoid the "It's not X, it's Y" sentence construction (e.g., "It's not that you stopped loving each other"). Rephrase to make the same point without the formulaic contrast.
